Human Trials - UDGJ#2

It was long time ago. Nobody remembers when and what was happened.

Now, humankind is nothing more than a test subject for an AI. You should find your way out by manipulating AI’s source code bit by bit.

Don’t forget; You have to die over and over!

I build this game for Unofficial Defold Game Jam#2. You can play on itch.io
